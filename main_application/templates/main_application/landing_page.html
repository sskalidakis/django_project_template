Django Project

Integrating Integrated Assessment Modelling in Support of the Agreement

Aiming to become the vessel of the international energy- and climate-economy modelling community, Django Project is an open-access, data exchange platform hosting detailed documentation, inputs, and outputs of modelling tools and exercises for all audiences

Modelling documentation

Detailed, dynamic, and comparative documentation of quantitative systems models in support of climate policy, regarding their representation of socioeconomics, sectors, policies, mitigation/adaptation measures, and emissions, as well as their geographic coverage

Workspaces of modelling exercises

Scientific interfaces, explanatory summaries for all audiences, along with customised visualisations of results, libraries with relevant content and downloadable datasets, heatmaps explaining how different models have been harmonised and/or interlinked, and open access to model inputs and outputs
